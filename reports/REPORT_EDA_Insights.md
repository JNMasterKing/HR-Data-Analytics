# 🕵️‍♂️ First Impressions: What the Data Told Us

This is the summary of my first look into the massive 2-million record HR dataset. Before I built any AI, I wanted to understand: *"Who are we as a company?"*

### 1. Our Workforce Story
- **We are a Tech Giant:** IT is our biggest department. If you're walking through the company, you're most likely to meet a **Software Engineer**.
- **Flexibility is Real:** 40% of our people work from home (**Remote**).
- **A Young Workforce:** Most of our people are in their first 4 years with us.

### 2. The Biggest Surprise
I looked at the relationship between how people perform and how much they get paid. **I found zero link.**
- Someone with a "5" rating (a superstar) might be paid less than someone with a "1" rating in the same role.
- **Why this matters:** This is a major "red flag." If we don't fix this pay-for-performance gap, our best people will feel undervalued.

### 3. Rapid Growth
By mapping out hiring dates, I saw a company that **exploded in size** after 2015. Managing this growth is the company's biggest challenge.
